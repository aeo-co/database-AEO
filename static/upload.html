Two file types are accepted, mixed in one batch:

  • AI visibility — .xlsx files named ClientName - Platform - YYYY-MM-DD.xlsx (e.g. Altenew - ChatGPT - 2026-07-21.xlsx). Platform is the AI tool queried: ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Mode.
  • Shopify report — {client}-all-data.csv files (e.g. outdoor-vitals-all-data.csv). These are the multi-section monthly report exports with === Section === blocks inside.
